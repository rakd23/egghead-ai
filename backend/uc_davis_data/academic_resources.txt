UC DAVIS ACADEMIC SUPPORT RESOURCES

TUTORING & LEARNING CENTERS

STUDENT ACADEMIC SUCCESS CENTER (SASC)
- Location: Dutton Hall 2205
- Free tutoring for many courses
- Drop-in and by-appointment options
- Subject areas: Math, sciences, writing, languages
- Study skills workshops
- Time management support
- Students say: Super helpful tutors, especially for STEM classes

WRITING SUPPORT

UNIVERSITY WRITING PROGRAM
- Location: Voorhies Hall
- One-on-one writing consultations
- Help with papers, essays, research projects
- Any stage of writing process
- Free for all students
- Students say: Helped improve writing significantly

STEM TUTORING

CHEMISTRY LEARNING CENTER
- Location: Chemistry Annex
- Drop-in tutoring for chemistry courses
- Peer tutors who took the same classes
- Practice problem sessions

PHYSICS TUTORING ROOM
- Location: Physics Building
- Drop-in help for physics courses
- TAs and peer tutors available

MATH TUTORIAL CENTER
- Drop-in tutoring for math courses
- Multiple locations

LIBRARIES

SHIELDS LIBRARY
- Main library
- Research help desk
- Study rooms you can reserve
- Computer labs
- Students say: Best place for serious studying, upper floors are quietest

PHYSICAL SCIENCES & ENGINEERING LIBRARY
- Located in Bainer Hall
- Specialized resources for STEM
- Great for engineering students

CARLSON HEALTH SCIENCES LIBRARY
- Medical/health sciences resources
- Good for pre-med, nursing, public health students

ACADEMIC ADVISING

COLLEGE ADVISING
- Every college has advisors
- Help with course planning, major requirements
- Declaration of major support
- Students say: Make appointments early, they book up fast

CAREER CENTER
- Location: Mrak Hall
- Resume help
- Interview prep
- Career counseling
- Job/internship search support
- Mock interviews available

OFFICE HOURS
- Every professor and TA has office hours
- GO TO THEM! Professors want to help
- Students say: Professors are way more helpful in office hours than you'd expect

ACADEMIC ACCOMMODATIONS

STUDENT DISABILITY CENTER (SDC)
- Location: Mark Hall
- Academic accommodations for students with disabilities
- Extended test time, note-taking support, etc.
- Need documentation to register

KEY POINTS:
- Most tutoring is FREE
- Don't wait until you're failing - get help early
- Office hours are underutilized - go to them!
- Shields Library study rooms book up fast during finals
