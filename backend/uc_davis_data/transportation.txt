UC DAVIS TRANSPORTATION

BIKES
- Davis is one of the most bike-friendly cities in the US
- Most students bike everywhere
- Bike circles (roundabouts) throughout campus
- Bring a GOOD bike lock - bike theft is common
- Students say: You NEED a bike to survive at Davis

UNITRANS
- Free bus system for students
- Runs throughout Davis and campus
- Multiple lines (A, B, C, D, E, F, G, J, K, L, M, N, O, P, Q, W, Z)
- Reduced service on weekends
- Download the Unitrans app for real-time tracking

ZIPCAR
- Car sharing service available on campus
- Good for grocery runs or trips to Sacramento
- Students with licenses can rent by the hour

YOLOBUS
- County bus system
- Connects Davis to Sacramento, Woodland, West Sacramento
- Some routes are free for students

AMTRAK
- Davis has an Amtrak station
- Capitol Corridor line runs to Sacramento, Bay Area
- Popular for going home on weekends

PARKING
- Limited on-campus parking
- Parking permits required and expensive
- Most students don't have cars on campus
- Students say: Don't bring a car freshman year
