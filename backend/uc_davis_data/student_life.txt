UC DAVIS STUDENT LIFE & TRADITIONS

PICNIC DAY
- Largest student-run event in the nation
- Held every April
- Campus opens to the public
- Parade, activities, exhibits, shows
- Battle of the Bands, Doxie Derby (dachshund races)
- Students say: Don't miss it! Super fun tradition

CLUBS & ORGANIZATIONS
- Over 800+ student organizations
- Greek life present but not dominant
- Many cultural, academic, and recreational clubs
- Club fair at the beginning of each quarter

AGGIE PACK
- Student spirit organization
- Attend sports games and show school spirit
- Free t-shirts and prizes

WHOLE EARTH FESTIVAL
- Three-day music and arts festival in May
- Free event on the Quad
- Live music, art, food vendors
- Students say: Great way to end the year

FARMERS MARKET
- Downtown Davis farmers market
- Wednesday evenings and Saturday mornings
- Great for fresh produce

DOWNTOWN DAVIS
- Small college town feel
- Restaurants, cafes, shops within biking distance
- G Street and surrounding areas

NOTABLE STUDENT TRADITIONS:
- Eggheads sculpture (campus landmark)
- Cow tipping jokes (don't actually do this)
- Aggie Blue and Gold school colors
- "Go Ags!" cheer
