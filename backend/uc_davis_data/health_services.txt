UC DAVIS HEALTH SERVICES

STUDENT HEALTH AND COUNSELING SERVICES (SHCS)

LOCATION & HOURS
- North Hall Addition
- Phone: (530) 752-2349
- Hours: Monday-Friday 8am-5pm
- After-hours nurse advice line: (530) 752-2300

MEDICAL SERVICES
- Primary care
- Urgent care
- Lab services
- X-rays
- Immunizations
- Travel medicine
- Sports medicine
- Physical therapy

INSURANCE
- UC SHIP (Student Health Insurance Plan)
- Most services covered or low co-pay
- Can waive if you have other insurance
- Students say: UC SHIP is comprehensive, worth keeping

PHARMACY
- On-site pharmacy at SHCS
- Prescription pickup
- Over-the-counter medications
- Students say: Convenient and usually cheaper than outside pharmacies

WOMEN'S HEALTH
- Gynecological exams
- Birth control
- STI testing and treatment
- Pregnancy testing

IMMUNIZATIONS & TB TESTING
- Required immunizations for students
- Flu shots (free)
- TB testing
- Travel vaccines

PHYSICAL THERAPY
- Sports injuries
- Injury rehabilitation
- Students say: Great for athletic injuries

NUTRITION COUNSELING
- Meet with registered dietitians
- Eating disorder support
- Sports nutrition
- Wellness nutrition

TELEHEALTH
- Virtual appointments available
- Good for minor issues, follow-ups
- Students say: Convenient when you can't make it to campus

MAKING APPOINTMENTS
- Call or use patient portal
- Urgent care: walk-ins accepted
- Regular appointments: may take a few days
- Students say: Book ahead for routine visits

HEALTH INSURANCE
- All students must have health insurance
- UC SHIP or private insurance
- Students say: Make sure your insurance is active before appointments

PRESCRIPTION REFILLS
- Request through patient portal
- Pick up at SHCS pharmacy
- Can transfer to outside pharmacy

CONFIDENTIALITY
- All medical records are confidential
- Privacy protected
- Parents cannot access records without permission (if you're 18+)
