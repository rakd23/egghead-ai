UC DAVIS KEY CAMPUS BUILDINGS

SHIELDS LIBRARY
- Main library on campus
- Multiple floors for studying
- Computer labs, group study rooms, quiet floors
- Open late during finals
- Students say: Best place for serious studying

THE SILO
- Student union building
- Food options, bookstore, meeting spaces
- Silo Market for grab-and-go food
- Popular hangout spot

ACTIVITIES AND RECREATION CENTER (ARC)
- Main gym and fitness center
- Pool, basketball courts, climbing wall, fitness classes
- Very popular with students
- Can get crowded in afternoons

MEMORIAL UNION (MU or "The Memo")
- Student center with Coffee House, meeting rooms
- LGBTQIA Resource Center
- Various student organization offices

THE QUAD
- Central grassy area surrounded by buildings
- Popular spot for studying outdoors, events
- Historic part of campus
