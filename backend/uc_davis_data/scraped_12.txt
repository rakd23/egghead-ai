Our Mission
Counseling Services provides student-centered mental health services, advocacy, outreach, and consultation to promote psychological well-being and academic success.
Our Values
Safety:
We create welcoming and confidential environments for student and community support.
Social Justice:
We value social justice through community engagement and challenging ourselves to fight inequality, marginalization, and oppression.
Development:
We support the development of our profession through training early career professionals and supporting the continued education of Counseling Services staff.
Best Practices:
We are committed to providing culturally responsive services rooted in best practices.
Collaboration:
We foster a community-wide approach to psychological well-being and collaborate with faculty, staff, and medical colleagues in this shared responsibility.
Personalized Goals:
We acknowledge that student success can be defined in many ways and we work with students on their paths to personal and academic goals.
Well-Being:
We believe the community we serve benefits from a commitment to Counseling Services staff well-being.
