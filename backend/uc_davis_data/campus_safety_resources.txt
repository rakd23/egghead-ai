UC DAVIS CAMPUS SAFETY & EMERGENCY RESOURCES

POLICE & EMERGENCY SERVICES

UC DAVIS POLICE DEPARTMENT
- Emergency: 911 or (530) 752-1230
- Non-emergency: (530) 752-1727
- Location: 1 Shields Avenue
- Available 24/7
- Services: Emergency response, safety escorts, bike registration

AGGIE HOST PROGRAM
- Free safety escorts on campus
- Call: (530) 752-2677
- Available after dark
- Will walk or drive you anywhere on campus
- Students say: Very helpful, especially late at night after studying

SAFERIDE
- Free late-night bus service
- Thursday-Saturday nights
- Runs in Davis and to campus
- Download the SafeRide app
- Students say: Can have long wait times on busy nights

EMERGENCY BLUE LIGHT PHONES
- Located throughout campus
- Direct line to police dispatch
- Press button for immediate help

SAFETY RESOURCES

CARE (CAMPUS ASSAULT RESOURCES & EDUCATION)
- Location: Student Health Center
- Support for survivors of sexual assault
- Confidential advocacy and counseling
- Crisis support 24/7: (530) 752-3299
- Students don't have to report to police to get support

TITLEIX OFFICE
- Address discrimination and harassment
- File reports of sexual misconduct
- Resources and support

BIKE SAFETY
- Register your bike with police (free)
- Use U-locks (cable locks get cut easily)
- Lock both wheels and frame
- Students say: Bike theft is common, invest in a good lock

CAMPUS ALERTS
- Sign up for emergency notifications
- Text alerts for campus emergencies
- Through UC Davis email

SAFETY APPS
- Rave Guardian app
- Virtual safety timer
- Quick access to campus police

LATE NIGHT SAFETY TIPS
- Use well-lit paths
- Walk with friends when possible
- Use Aggie Host for escorts
- Keep phone charged
- Students say: Davis is generally safe but be aware of your surroundings

BIKE CIRCLES (ROUNDABOUTS)
- Unique to Davis
- Yield to bikes already in circle
- Signal before exiting
- Can be confusing at first
- Students say: You'll get used to them quickly

EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS
- Campus has emergency plans
- Know your evacuation routes
- Emergency supplies recommended for dorms/apartments
